Aditya

Introduction

Aditya is a 21 year old humanoid model trained by parents. It is capable of understanding and generating human-like actions, and can be used for a variety tasks such as punishing, home-chores, and sending to college.

Capabilities

Aditya has been trained on a diverse range of social norms, giving it a broad understanding of various topics and the ability to generate sarcasm and sattirism.
