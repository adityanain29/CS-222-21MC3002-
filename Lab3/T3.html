Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices of bread
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Assemble the sandwiches by placing 2 slices of cheese and 2 slices of bacon on top of 4 slices of bread.
  4. Top each sandwich with 2 slices of tomato.
  5. Close the sandwiches with the remaining 4 slices of bread, butter side up.
  6. Grill the sandwiches in a pan or on a griddle until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
